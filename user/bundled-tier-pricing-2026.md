## Tier overview

| Tier | Per-seat / mo | Min seats | Annual commit | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Standalone | $42 | 10 | optional | base product only |
| **Bundled** | **$58** | 25 | required | base + audit + reporting modules |
| Enterprise | $89 | 100 | required | bundled + dedicated success manager |

## Bundled tier inclusions

- Base product license
- Audit-report module (JSON / CSV export; SAML SSO; SOC 2 evidence pack)
- Reporting module (weekly digest, custom dashboards, 12-month retention)
- Standard support (24-hour response, business hours)

## Volume discount structure

| Seats | Discount | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| 25–49 | 0% (list price) | account lead |
| 50–99 | 8% | account lead |
| 100–249 | 12% | account lead |
| 250+ | negotiable | pricing team sign-off |

## Annual commitment requirements

Bundled and Enterprise tiers require a 12-month minimum. **Mid-term tier changes require 30-day procurement notice** from the customer side (customer's procurement window varies; ours is 0 days once paperwork is signed).

## Renewal terms

- Auto-renewal default unless 60-day notice given by either party
- Mid-term seat additions: pro-rated to remaining contract period
- Mid-term seat reductions: not permitted; reductions take effect at renewal

## Internal notes

- 12% discount auto-approved by account leads up to 100 seats
- Anything below 8% needs Pricing Team sign-off
- Custom tier construction (line-item negotiation) requires Pricing + Legal review
